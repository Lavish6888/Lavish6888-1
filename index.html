Load testing doesn't have to be difficult.

Traditional performance testing has always happened in the protocol layer. This results in massive tests that are hard to read, require extensive programming, and take hours, even weeks to set up. Even the results aren’t even indicative of the real world as they test the protocol.

No wonder performance testing seemed hard.

But it doesnt have to be with LoadNinja. Instead of testing the protocol, test the actual end-user experience across tens of thousands of browsers using our TrueLoad technology, saving you over 60% of effort. No maintenance or setup required to give you the most accurate understanding of performance in the least amount of time possible.

Efficient: Record & Instantly Replay Load Tests

Cut web and API test creation time from hours to minutes. No coding or correlation needed, even with the most complex transactions using the AI enhanced InstaPlay recorder.

Accurate: Load Test With Real Browsers at Scale

Generate the most realistic load possible from different regions of the world with tens of thousands of real browsers that accurately represents your end users. No more maintaining and setting up load emulators.

Actionable: Metrics that Matter

Analyze performance data like step times, resources processed by the browser, asynchronous calls and the associated navigation timings across your web UI and API layer. Quickly diagnose performance issues with a machine-learning assistant.
